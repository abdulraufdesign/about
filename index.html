Outside of My Canvas

I love to organize things. People say I am really great at it. I use design thinking and layouts to help myself and others organize their work and lives. I also have a strong passion for productivity systems. The biggest power? I design with heart and precision, blending creativity with systems thinking to make experiences that feel as good as they work.

I’m always capturing thoughts — in journals, loose notes, screenshots, and half-sketched diagrams. I love exploring ideas, connecting dots between things I read, and returning to them later to refine, question, and build on them. A lot of that exploration centers around space and history — how we got here, how systems evolve, and what might come next. That curiosity leaks directly into my work: I design like a researcher and a builder at the same time. I zoom out to understand the bigger story, then zoom in to obsess over the tiny details that make an experience feel intentional, clear, and genuinely useful.
