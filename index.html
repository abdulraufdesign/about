My story

I started my journey 5 years ago when I created my first ever YouTube channel. I used to edit and upload gaming videos there. After that, I got deep into video editing, and started an Instagram page where I used to upload some crazy 2D animations inspired by @y6richo.
I was greatly inspired by Google's Material Design, so I started learning the fundamentals of design, typography rules, and eventually got fall in love with the UI Design.
I learned to use Figma, created some really messy website designs, and improved my design methodologies.
Previously, I used to think that UX is all about creating user interfaces until I found GFXMentor on YouTube.
I finally became a UX Designer in 2023, since then, I am seeking new opportunites to excel my career in the UX Design industry, and I love it.

I believe that great design is born from empathy, collaboration, and experimentation, and I strive to bring these values to every project I work on. Whether it's conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, or testing and iterating on designs, I am committed to delivering high-quality solutions that meet the needs of both users and stakeholders.
